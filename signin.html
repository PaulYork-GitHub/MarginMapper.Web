FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Margin Mapper?

Margin Mapper is a tool that integrates with Jira and other cloud platforms for Time records and invoicing information, to help businesses track project costs, effort, income, margin, and overall profit or loss in real time.

How does Margin Mapper retrieve financial data?

Margin Mapper pulls time-tracking data from services like Jira and Tempo, and reads information from your invoicing platform then calculates financial metrics based on predefined cost and billing rates for your team.

Can I use Margin Mapper with my existing Jira setup?

Absoutley, Margin Mapper works with your existing Jira projects. You'll authenticate using Jira OAuth, and the system will fetch relevant project data automatically.

Is there a free version of Margin Mapper?

Yes! Margin Mapper offers a freemium model where you can access basic tracking features for free. Advanced reporting and premium insights are available in the paid version.
